Welcome To My Drone Page

Welcome to my page about one of my hobbies, drones! I have been interested in flying drones to capture content since I was a kid, and I finally got the chance to do so after I purchased my first drone 3 years ago. My first drone was the DJI Mini 2, which is a great beginner drone to get into. After using it for 3 years I finally switched to the DJI Mini 3 Pro which is my current drone. I talk more about how they compare in their separate pages. I also separately present some content that I took with each of them. The images have been compressed to have better webpage performance. There is a lot to talk about with the different accessories and controllers so you can find more about them in the dedicated pages. I also have an Instagram page which is about photography, and I share photos I captured on my drones. I also have some future plans that I shared in its dedicated page. I hope you enjoy the content and if you have feedback you can fill out the form.
